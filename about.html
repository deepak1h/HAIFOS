Establishment initiative

HAIFOS Innovative Forum is a Non-Profit Company registered under section 8 of the 2013 company Act on September 29, 2023. This organization is a new idea of providing digital literacy in remote areas, born out three years ago during the struggle for the people residing in the flood prone area of Koshi river (Bihar). The struggle was to provide education, dignity, rehabilitation and resettlement to those affected by the flood.

However, our journey began within the campus of IITH, from 2022. During our visit to the peripheral school of telangana as students under our course work, we observed that despite the presence of digital equipment in some of these schools, a lack of digital knowledge among students. It also encourages us to make a group who are eager to use their outstanding knowledge for the development of the society. Some of our Professors help us in this initiative.

We believe that our small effort will contribute towards reducing digital divide, inequalities and provide a ‘level playing field’ to all sections of the society. Our ethos revolves around honesty, transparency and deep commitment to build a just and fair society, where every individual has equal opportunity to thrive.


Governance


The organization has a General Board Assembly consisting of founding members and other members, who have joined the association after its establishment to oversee and deal with all policy issues. The Managing Director is responsible to the Board of directors,and is entrusted with the main task of overseeing and implementing decisions passed by the General Body and Board of Directors. She also manages the day-to-day activities and operations of the organization.
